About Williamson Transport

Jerald Williamson

Jerald Williamson was born on December 24, 1984. He grew up in a small house with his mother, father, and older brother. As a child he was always fascinated with cars and driving.

At age 16, when Jerald acquired his license, he realized driving was what he wanted to do with his life. Years later he amassed jobs for companies like Coca-Cola, Best Buy, and Kroger. At all of these jobs he wanted to do the same thing- drive. The thrill and devotion he had to the activity was unmatched. Everyone knew it was his passion and what he wanted to do in life, but all he really wanted to do was make himself proud and determine his own definition of success.

Finally, in 2019 he built up the courage and resources to pursue his dream. Buying a truck and getting his name out there were his first steps. At first business was slow. Now business couldn't be better and he's found his definition of success.

Williamson Transport

Williamson Transport is a small organization that provides transportation of goods and services to its customers. In order to better serve these customers, Jerald Wlliamson, the owner of the company, makes all deliveries using his truck. Despite being a small organization, Williamson Transport has delivered goods to many large corporations.

Williamson Transport was started in 2019 by Jerald Williamson. After working as a driver for many other companies, Williamson bought a truck of his own and started making deliveries. The company is owned and operated by Williamson alone. Any business needs or inquiries are handled by Williamson. At Williamson Transport, we strive to provide the best quality service to our customers.
